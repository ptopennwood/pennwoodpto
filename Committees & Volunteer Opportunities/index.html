Committees & Volunteer Opportunities

Questions? Email: pennwoodpto@yahoo.com

SCHOOL WIDE

Field Day - Held in June. Assist Administration by organizing volunteers to help with water stations throughout the day. Visit tents throughout the day to make sure everything is running smoothly.

Faculty Breakfast - Held in October. Help plan and organize the breakfast for teachers and faculty. Create a Sign-Up Genius to obtain food donations & volunteers to help with set-up and clean-up. Purchase any items that are not donated. Leftovers are saved for teachers to eat throughout the day.

Ice Cream Summer Reading Social - Event held in September. Coordinate with Administration on number of students, date/time and what needs to be purchased for the social. If volunteers are needed a signup genius can be created.

Holiday Gifts for Support Staff/Custodians - December - Purchase gift cards for Secretaries in the main office. Purchase either cookie trays or pizza & soda for Custodial Staff.

Faculty Appreciation Lunch - Usually held in May. Visit local delis asking if they can work within our budget and provide quantity of food needed. Create a Sign-Up Genius asking for volunteers and food donations. There are 3 lunch times - donated items need to be split so there is variety/freshness at each lunch. Clean up and consolidate leftovers and leave for teachers to eat during the day.

Walk-a-thon - Held in October. Coordinate parent helpers via Sign-Up Genius to hand out water during the walk-a-thon. Help with prizes the following week.

Evening Socials - Coordinate with Student Council as they run these events. Obtain food and volunteers to help with the event. Run food station

GRADE LEVEL

6th Grade Picnic - Held the first full day of school. Coordinate with administration on number of 6th grade students Purchase supplies needed. Create a Sign-Up Genius to obtain volunteers to help run the event.

8th Grade Happening - Held in June. Help plan, organize activities, prepare the Sign-Up Genius to obtain food donations and volunteers to help run the event. Decide on a theme and create subcommittes to help with the event; tickets, food, decorations etc.

WE NEED YOUR HELP

Getting involved is a great way to show your kids that you take an interest in their education and consider school a worthwhile cause. It's also a great way to better know the teachers and staff at our school. The PTO needs volunteers during the school year to handle specific tasks or assist with events. Some tasks can even be done at home. You can check our Sign-Up Genius to see ongoing volunteer opportunities that would fit your schedule. Please help support Pennwood by volunteering for the PTO.

Our continued success depends on you!

FUNDRAISERS

Scholastic Book Fair - Held in the Fall. Coordinate with Scholastic on delivery and pickup from Pennwood. Obtain volunteers to work in shifts & help with setup, sales and break down.

Barnes & Noble Night - Takes place in May. Coordinate with Faculty Advisor on scheduling date and planned activities for the night. Organize sign up genius for volunteers needed for the night

Spirit Wear - Help kick off the school year with our school pride sale. Secure vendor and manage order procedures and delivery of items. If needed, recruit volunteers.

Box Tops - Collect box tops from the PTO mailbox and submit them to earn funds for PW. Work with the PTO to run contests throughout the year to help boost box tops submissions. This can be done from home.

McCaffrey's - Collect receipts every other month. Add up the receipts. Place the receipts and completed form in an envelope and bring it to McCaffrey's Customer Service Desk. This can be done from home.

Baskin Robbins Night - Work with local store and Administration to set a date to promote event.

Giant/Target - Both stores offer programs that earn funds for PW. Funds from these programs benefit the Pennwood Student Activities Account. Promote program to increase enrollment by registering reward/credit cards for these stores. Periodically check in on programs to verify status and fund disbursements to the school. This can be done from home.

Magazine Drive - Work with Administrator on coordinating sale kickoff and collection dates. Secures volunteers to help with tallying orders and money. Distributes prizes to students. Event typically runs in October. Funds benefit the Pennwood Student Activities Account.
