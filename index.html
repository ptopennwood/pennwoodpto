Announcements/News

  • VOLUNTEERS NEEDED

    We are still looking for a few parents to chair committees this year. Please click here for a list of open positions and add your name if interested.

    The Book Fair will be held 12/5-12/9 and will end with Family Fun Night. Your help is needed to make this event a success!

  • COOKIE DOUGH DELIVERY - Cookie Dough will be delivered on Thursday, December 17th. Orders will be sent home with your student unless they are to heavy to be transported safely. You will be able to pick up in the SDR until 6pm. Please coordinate with your Student.
  • FAMILY FUN NIGHT - We are excited to announce our 2nd Annual Family Fun Night on December 9th! Join us for a night of raffles, dodgeball tournament, vendors and more! If you would like to reserve a vendor table please complete the registration found here. We look forward to seeing you!
  • BOX TOPS - The November Box Tops contest has begun! The Homeroom that turns in the most Box Tops wins a pizza party! We have already collected almost 2,000 Box Tops in November! Keep clipping and Good Luck!
  • FAMILY PAINT NIGHT - We are in the planning stages for this Fun event! Stay tuned for more information!
  • SPIRIT WEAR - We will have Pennsbury socks, hats, magnets and water bottles available on 12/9 during Fun Night. Thank you for your support!
  • GYM UNIFORMS - If your student is having an issue with their Gym Uniform contact us and we can help with an exchange.
  • "PRINCIPAL APPROVED" WATER BOTTLES - Pennsbury water bottles are still available! Contact any member of the Executive Board if interested. This is an optional PTO fundraiser and not a requirement. Any clear water bottles are permitted. Thank you for your support!
  • CAR MAGNET SALE - The PTO is selling Pennwood car magnets for $5.00 throughout the school year. Click here to print the order form or you may order electronically via Paypal.
  • SCHOOL ARRIVAL INFORMATION - The School Day begins at 8:10 AM and ends 2:55 PM. Students should not arrive at school before 7:45 AM. To review the TEMPORARY Student drop off and pick up traffic pattern please click here.
  • RENOVATION NEWS - Mrs. Aldridge has started an informative and comprehensive Blog to keep the Pennwood Community updated as we move forward with the extensive renovation.
  • PENNWOOD MONTHLY NEWSLETTER - Catch up on past events and be informed on upcoming events by reviewing the June Newsletter.
  • DAILY BULLETIN - Do you want to know what the student announcements are each morning? Find out here!
  • SUPERINTENDENT'S NEWSLETTER - The November issue of This Month In Pennsbury is now available.
  • VOLUNTEER POLICY - For information about the volunteer policy for the 2016/2017 school year click here.
  • LATE BUSES - There are late buses daily at 5:15. There are additional late buses on Tuesday and Thursday at 4:05. Students may stay after school for special help, make-up work, clubs, sports activities, office detention and library use. Students must be under direct supervision of a teacher when staying beyond 2:55 pm. If student athletes return after 5:15 pm, they must provide their own transportation.
