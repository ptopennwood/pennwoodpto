Announcements/News

  • VOLUNTEERS NEEDED

    Interested in chairing a Committee next year? We need many helping hands to make the 2017/2018 school year a success! A complete list can be found here.

  • CHROMEBOOKS - Beginning this Summer, Students currently in grade 5-11 will be issued a Chromebook as part of the District's Future Ready initiative. For the most up to date information and to find answers to many of your questions please visit the dedicated Chromebook website which can be found here.
  • BOX TOPS - The Box Tops committee is rolling the Duct Tape contest Box Tops into this month's competition for Field Day points. Keep Clipping!
  • IMMUNIZATIONS - Immunization laws have changed for the 2017/2018 School Year. School Vaccination requirements can be found here.
  • SPIRIT WEAR - The next opportunity to purchase Spirit Wear will be during the 6th Grade Picnic. Water bottles, magnets, knit hats/visors and socks will be available.
  • "PRINCIPAL APPROVED" WATER BOTTLES - Pennsbury water bottles are still available! Contact any member of the Executive Board if interested. This is an optional PTO fundraiser and not a requirement. Any clear water bottles are permitted. Thank you for your support!
  • CAR MAGNET SALE - The PTO is selling Pennwood car magnets for $5.00 throughout the school year. Click here to print the order form or you may order electronically via Paypal.
  • PAVER PROJECT - Quarry Hill invites Alumni to have their name inscribed on a commemorative paver in the The Learning Nest. Additional information can be found here.
  • SCHOOL ARRIVAL INFORMATION - The School Day begins at 8:10 AM and ends 2:55 PM. Students should not arrive at school before 7:45 AM. To review the TEMPORARY Student drop off and pick up traffic pattern please click here.
  • RENOVATION NEWS - Mrs. Aldridge has started an informative and comprehensive Blog to keep the Pennwood Community updated as we move forward with the extensive renovation.
  • DAILY BULLETIN - Do you want to know what the student announcements are each morning? Find out here!
  • SUPERINTENDENT'S NEWSLETTER - The May issue of This Month In Pennsbury is now available.
  • VOLUNTEER POLICY - For information about the volunteer policy for the 2017/2018 school year click here.
  • LATE BUSES - There are late buses daily at 5:15. There are additional late buses on Tuesday and Thursday at 4:05. Students may stay after school for special help, make-up work, clubs, sports activities, office detention and library use. Students must be under direct supervision of a teacher when staying beyond 2:55 pm. If student athletes return after 5:15 pm, they must provide their own transportation. Both late bus schedules can be found here.
