Announcements/News

  • VOLUNTEERS NEEDED

    Interested in chairing a Committee this year? We need many helping hands to make the school year a success! A complete list can be found here.

  • SPIRIT WEAR - Spirit Wear forms will be sent home with your Student on the first day of school. Orders are due no later than September 25th. Sample sizes will be available during Back To School Night and the Welcome Picnic. Thank you for your support!
  • KIDSTUFF BOOKS - Coupon books will be coming home with your Student the first day of school. Orders and books (if you do not wish to purchase) must be returned by September 15th.
  • FAMILY PAINT NIGHT - Join us on October 20th for Family Paint Night! Create your own masterpiece led by artists from Art In The Pod! Reserve your space by signing up here.
  • BOX TOPS - Keep collecting for the Summer BoxTop contest! For every 10 Boxtops you turn in you will earn an entry for the Fall Gift Basket raffle! The winner will be announced on September 14th at the 7th/8th Grade Back To School Night
  • CHROMEBOOKS - You should have picked up your Chromebooks by now. Students are encouraged to decorate the cases. If you were not able to pick up within the distribution window you will be able to get one after school starts. Please email Kevin Dorsey-kdorsey@pennsburysd.org. If you will not be participating in the program Students will be required to sign one in and out of the Library each day. Please visit the dedicated Chromebook website which can be found here.
  • GYM UNIFORMS - The second wave of Gym Uniform ordering is underway. Order by August 31st to ensure delivery by September 11th. You will also be able to purchase uniforms at Back To School Night.
  • VOLUNTEER POLICY - Volunteer clearances are sent from the feeder elementary schools to Pennwood. Parents that are new to the District or did not complete them while in elementary school need to complete the paperwork and clearances. The requirements are included here. Volunteers need to complete an affidavit each year. Those forms are in the office. If you sign up to volunteer at an event that requires clearances we will you contact prior to the event if additional documentation is needed - including the affidavit.
  • HOMEROOM ASSIGNMENTS - Assignments and Bus Schedules will be available via the Home Access Center on August 25th after 4pm. Supply lists can be found here on the bottom left under PW Information.
  • SCHOOL ARRIVAL INFORMATION - The School Day begins at 8:10 AM and ends 2:55 PM. Students should not arrive at school before 7:45 AM. To review the Student drop off and pick up traffic pattern please click here.
  • CAR MAGNET SALE - The PTO is selling Pennwood car magnets for $5.00 throughout the school year. Click here to print the order form or you may order electronically via Paypal.
  • DAILY BULLETIN - Do you want to know what the student announcements are each morning? Find out here!
  • RENOVATION NEWS - Mr. Majikas has an informative and comprehensive Blog to keep the Pennwood Community updated as we move forward with the extensive renovation.
  • SUPERINTENDENT'S NEWSLETTER - The August issue of This Month In Pennsbury is now available.
  • PARENT/GUARDIAN HANDBOOK - The 2017/2018 Handbook can be found here.
  • LATE BUSES - There are late buses daily at 5:15. There are additional late buses on Tuesday and Thursday at 4:05. Students may stay after school for special help, make-up work, clubs, sports activities, office detention and library use. Students must be under direct supervision of a teacher when staying beyond 2:55 pm. If student athletes return after 5:15 pm, they must provide their own transportation. Both late bus schedules can be found here.
